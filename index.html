The mission

Build something worth sharing.

Make something that raises awareness for one of our non-profits — something they could send straight to their donors and wider audience to spark real engagement. We've kept the brief wide open on purpose: this is about creativity, not building internal tools.

Over the Moon

What if nothing was ever just rubbish?

Who they helpFamilies, children and adults across Adur and Brighton — and the planet.
The problemUseful materials get binned instead of reused, and getting creative can be costly.
Their solutionScrapstores that redistribute donated materials cheaply, with low-cost spaces to make and reuse.
The mission Get the public to think twice before binning something — to reuse or donate it instead.
Link to website →

Idea sparks

A fresh website to replace their current one An app that reveals the hidden value of your scrap Reuse-or-donate, made playful

Figment Arts

Art that gives a voice to those too often left out.

Who they helpLearning-disabled, autistic and neurodivergent adults, plus families facing hardship in coastal West Sussex.
The problemNeighbourhoods with little access to arts and culture, where voices too often go unheard.
Their solutionSupported studios, free workshops and exhibitions where people make art about their own lives.
The mission Spread the word about their Crowdfunder to build a permanent home of their own — a dedicated art studio for the community.
Link to Crowdfunder → Link to website →

Shaped with David at Figment Arts

  1. A responsive feedback wall, where supporters and audiences can post their thoughts in a visually engaging way.
  2. An embeddable fundraising thermometer, live-linked to the Crowdfunder to show progress toward the target.

Apron Creative

The best things grow when we grow them together.

Who they helpThe diverse local community around Lancing and Shoreham — beginning with families.
The problemToo few local spaces to get creative outdoors, connect and grow — on land left disused.
Their solutionTurning neglected land into thriving community gardens, and now a farm, for food, skills and nature.
The mission Raise awareness of their Crowdfunder to create a regenerative community farm.
Link to Crowdfunder → Link to website →

Idea sparks

Get people excited about community farming Make biodiversity tangible Climate resilience, told playfully

What we're looking for

Bring ideas, not perfection.

We're not expecting complex or fully-formed apps. What matters most is a fresh idea — and a simple, working prototype of the one key feature that brings it to life.
